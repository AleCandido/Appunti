\section*{Abstract}
The problem is to diagonalise the Ising Hamiltonian in a transverse magnetic field, \cref{IsingH}, with fermionic operators.

The Hamiltonian is the following:

\begin{equation}
H = - \Gamma \sum_i S_i^z - J \sum_i S_i^x S_{i+1}^x
\label{IsingH}
\end{equation}
